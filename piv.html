PI&V.

who I am

I see design as a way of making interactions and objects more intentional. I am interested in where physical making, digital systems, and human behaviour meet.

My style is fairly direct: I like working through ideas quickly, testing them through prototypes, and improving them through iteration.

direction

What I value

Clarity

I like things that are understandable, legible, and honest in what they do.

Iteration

Good ideas improve through testing, adjusting, and making again.

position

What I am moving toward

I want to develop toward a practice where concept thinking and physical execution strengthen each other, rather than existing separately.
